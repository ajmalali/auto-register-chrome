How to use the extension

Step 1: Get your CRNs

Go to KFUPM Scheduler and generate your schedule. Copy your CRNs by clicking on the Copy CRNs button.

Note: Getting CRNs from KFUPM scheduler only makes the process easier for you. You may get your CRNs however you like.

Step 2: Enter your CRNs

Paste your CRNs into the input box (no need to edit it). if you did not use KFUPM scheduler, you can enter each CRN one by one or all together separated by a space.

Step 3: Add your CRNs

Add your CRNs to Main CRNs if you want them to be registered first. These will be your primary choice of courses.

Add your CRNs to Backup CRNs if you want them to be registered in case you expect your Main CRN course to get closed. Adding Backup CRNs is optional.

Step 4: All set

When it's time for registration, go to the Register classes (Add/Drop) page, select the term and let the extension do its work.

What the extension does

When the Register classes page opens, the extension will first automatically fill the Main CRNs and submit. After the page reloads, the extension will fill the Backup CRNs (if any) and submit it again.

You will (obviously) get a registration error if one of the Main CRNs course gets registered and the Backup CRNs tries to register the same course. You can safely ignore the error as it will not cause a registration hold.
